Cinema Arcana

Tarot For Movies

A tarot reading that ends in a film. The app maps your mood, colors it with a three-card draw, and scours the whole history of cinema for the one movie that matches.

How it works

1. Set six mood dials — or type what you want to feel — or pick up to four films you love and let the app read your taste from them.

2. Draw a three-card spread (Past, Present, Future). The cards nudge the reading.

3. Receive your projection: one film, an alignment reading, and runner-ups.

Reroll for a different pick, Pass to rule one out, and log favorites to your journal. There's also a daily guess-the-film game.
